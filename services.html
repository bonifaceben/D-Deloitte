What We Offer

Sales & Supply of All Electrical Accessories, Equipment and Appliances

We supply trusted, durable electrical products tailored to residential, commercial and industrial needs — from everyday essentials to large-scale equipment.

Sales of Electrical Accessories

We offer a wide range of high-quality electrical accessories including switches, sockets, cables, plugs, connectors, and other essential components. Our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers to ensure durability, safety, and reliability.

Supply of Electrical Equipment

We supply industrial and commercial electrical equipment such as transformers, circuit breakers, distribution boards, panels, and control systems. Our solutions are tailored to meet the needs of construction projects, industries, and businesses.

Sales of Electrical Appliances

We provide modern and energy-efficient electrical appliances for residential and commercial use, including lighting systems, fans, air conditioning units, and other essential appliances designed for comfort and performance.

Marine Services

Marine Equipment, LSA/FFA Supply, Recertification & Hull Gauging

Beyond electrical products, we supply and service marine safety equipment for vessels, jetties and offshore facilities — keeping operators safe and compliant.

Marine Equipment, LSA & FFA

Supply of marine equipment, Life Saving Appliances (LSA) and Fire Fighting Appliances (FFA) including life rafts, life rings, fire hose reels, EPIRBs and distress signals for vessels and offshore facilities.

Recertification of LSA & FFA

Inspection, servicing and recertification of life-saving and fire-fighting appliances — including hydrostatic release units — to keep your vessel safe and compliant with marine safety standards.

Hull Gauging Services

Accurate hull thickness gauging and reporting to support vessel safety, maintenance planning and class surveys.
